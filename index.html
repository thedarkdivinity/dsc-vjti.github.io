About The Community

Who we are and What we do!

Our Motto

Through the DSC community, We want to foster the ability of giving back to the society with the help of technology It's a club where we learn and create amazing stuff together with other inquisitive developers and exhilarate everyone to their core. The motive is to create a local ecosystem of Developers in and around the Campus.

About VJTI

Located in South Mumbai, VJTI is an autonomous institution owned by Maharashtra State Government. VJTI is known for its high-quality teaching, collaborative research, industry connect, and strong alumni network. VJTI is also the Central Technical Institute of Maharashtra State. The institute trains students in engineering and technology at the certificate,diploma, degree, post-graduate and doctoral levels.

Technologies

Domains we are excited to collaborate and teach!

Web Development

Web development is the building and maintenance of websites, it's the work that happens behind the scenes to develop a website connecting the frontend and the Backend and provide a seamless user experience. Get access to tutorials and hands-on coding experience.

App Development

Want to create your own apps? Every year Google Developers release exciting new updates to the world's most popular operating system. We keep study jams and provide resources for App Development Languages to help master the latest trends in android development.

Cloud Computing

Every time you send a file via the web, use a mobile app, binge a show, or play an online video game. All these services are stored in the cloud and exist in some digital space. We conduct Google Cloud Program to understand the concepts of Cloud with Qwiklabs.

Machine Learning

Google's Explore ML Program comming soon! Learn how to drive user engagement and retention with intelligent apps that are able to effectively serve users with the ability to automatically learn and improve from experience.

Open Source

Open Source is contributing to codebases and applying your knowledge of technical concepts to projects available freely on the internet. Learn how to contribute and apply to various open source programs of GSOC, MLH, Outreachy and many more!

Graphic Designing

Designing posters is an integral part of communicating about the events and workshops to the students. It is the art of creating visual content for communication purposes. Think you can contribute to DSC this way? Well, come on in!

Frequently Asked Questions

Need help? We've got you covered!

Who can become a member of DSC?

Students from all undergraduate or graduate programs with an interest in growing as a developer are welcome.

Any prerequisites for joining DSC?

Only your interest and will to learn. You will find an atmosphere to foster and learn from basics to advanced skills.

Why DSC when we have a similar club?

DSC intends to serve the society and create an impact through technology. DSC would be representing our college on the National level in various coding contests and hackathons. DSC is open to inter college collaborations as well.

What opportunities does DSC provide to us?

We're a hub of coders and programmers who help solve coding problems, develop new skills and projects, and enhance your career path.

Are DSC activities free of cost?

We are a non profit organization. All events and workshops conducted by DSC are free of cost and open for all to attend and learn from.

I want to be a part of the core team. How can I contribute to the working and organization of this community?

We are excited to onboard students to the team of DSC. Feel free to approach us personally with the sector you would want to work for.
